This tool is made by a student, for other students, its not perfect but hopefully it can help. Obviously this website is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by The University of Sydney.

Scoring Criteria

The remaining possible timetables are scored based on:
  • Amount of days that contain a class. Timetables that require to attend the least amount of days score higher.
  • The start time for each day. Earlier starts are scored lower than later starts. (NOTE: The finish time for each day is not considered when scoring.)
  • Timetables that lump your lectures into one day are more favourable than having your lectures spread out over the week. This is so this day may be skipped if needed and the lectures can be watched online.
You are only shown the highest scored as there may be hundreds of possible combinations.
